Visit The Andaman Islands!

The Andaman Islands are an Indian archipelago in the Bay of Bengal. There are so many differnt things you can do there. From swimming in the beautiful blue water to going on elephant rides, there is something for everyone. The Andaman Islands are a perfect vaction spot for you and your family. Keep reading to learn more about these gorgeous beaches.


Even though the Andaman Islands are small, there are so many different activities you can do there!

Tourists Attractions You Can Visit:

  • Ride Elephants on Havelock Island. Havelock Island is part of the Andaman Islands. Its know for many things, especially its elephant rides. Ride Elephants along the coast of the shore. It is extremely popular and a perfect spot to watch the sunset from.
  • Swimming and Diving on Radhangar beach. Explore the beautiful waters at Radhangar beach. You can see the coral and all the creatures that live in the ocean.
  • Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park. This Park is located near Wandoor on the Andaman Islands. There, you will be able to see many wonderful and pretty creatures.
  • Chidiya Tapu. This attraction is famous for its birdwatching and sunset viewing, with a nearby mini zoo.
  • Limestone Cave. Walk around in th Limestone Cave and see all of the beautiful Architecture and artifacts.
  • Viper Island. Viper Island is an Island of the Adaman Islands. There a former sites with historic ruins located there.
